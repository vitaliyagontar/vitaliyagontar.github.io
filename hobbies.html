Hobbies & Interests

Interests

When it comes to how I spend my free time, it sounds pretty much ordinary: watching series and movies or reading books. I have already watched over 20 different series (you can check them here),and the list is only expanding. Mostly I watch them while eating, although I am aware that it is thought to be not healthy and good for us.

However, when it actually comes to quality free time I prefer reading books. Mostly this is fiction and sometimes non-fiction; usually they are in Russian language. My goal is to one day push myself to read books (not textbooks or articles) in other languages. I strongly believe that reading is what makes us actually think and broaden our mind, imagination and inner world. Choosing a right book is indeed difficult, but it's worth looking for.

Certainly, as any normal person, most of my free time actually is spent with my family and friends. Unfortunately, they are now spread around the world, and it is getting harder to meet with them regularly :(

My other passion is driving cars. In my childhood I was playing mostly with cars and was dreaming about the day when I will be able to drive my own one. Thus, getting driving license was one of the happiest moments in my life. I still do not have my own car, but I often take my parents' ones; luckily, they have stick in their cars, which I strongly prefer. It is simply something in the way car drives, the way I can lead it, the way they sound and the feeling I get when I am at the wheel.

Another thing I am passionate about is food :) I eat almost everything and like to try out something unknown, as well. I love both, eating and trying food in cafes and restaurants and cooking by myself at home. We are what we eat; thus, I do pay a lot of attention to food.

Finally, it can hardly be called a hobby or actual interest, but whoever knows me more than an hour most likely knows that I am fond of dogs. Since my childhood I always wanted one, I have learned (seems to be naturally though) the majority of the breeds and everything about dogs. I had an amazing experience in volunteering in one of the shelters in Klaipeda, Lithuania (look how great it was!), where I could actually help those without proper home, in any way: from cleaning their crates and yard to walking and simply spending time together with them. I hugely appreciate this experience and consider doing this on a regular basis.

Travelling

This is what I am eager to spend most of my money for and what really inspires me in lots of different ways. I have already been to 16 different countries and have plans for dozens more. I simply think the world is amazingly different everywhere (especially in terms of nature), and for me it is worth seeing as much as possible.

Sometimes I need to escape form daily routine, to see new horizons; sometimes I am dying to go skiing and visit mountains; recently I really enjoy having adventures and fun with my friends while traveling; once or twice per year I travel with my family and it's quite bonding and fun, as well. Purposes and reasons differ almost every time, but what stays the same is an enormous amount of experience, feelings, new stories and definitely insipartion for the near future.
